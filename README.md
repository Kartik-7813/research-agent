# 📚 AI Research Agent

## Overview

AI Research Agent is an intelligent research assistant built using **IBM watsonx Orchestrate**. It helps researchers, students, and professionals quickly retrieve information from a curated research knowledge base and transform it into structured research insights.

Instead of manually reading multiple research papers and technical documents, users can ask research-related questions and receive concise literature reviews, key findings, common themes, and future research directions through an interactive AI-powered interface.

---

## Features

* 🔍 Retrieve relevant information from uploaded research documents.
* 📄 Generate concise literature reviews.
* 💡 Extract key findings and common research themes.
* 🚀 Suggest future research directions.
* 💬 Interactive AI chat interface embedded into a responsive web application.
* 📚 Powered by a curated research knowledge base.

---

## System Workflow

1. User enters a research-related query.
2. The AI Research Companion searches the uploaded knowledge base.
3. Relevant research information is retrieved.
4. The LLM analyzes and synthesizes the retrieved content.
5. The system generates:

   * Relevant Research Sources
   * Literature Review
   * Key Findings
   * Common Themes
   * Future Research Directions
